We Do Need AN Education!

Who are we?

The Union of High-School Students of North Macedonia (Сојуз на средношколци на Република Северна Македонија – ССРСМ / Unioni i Nxënësve të Shkollave të Mesme – UNSHM) is the only nationwide representative body of high-school students in the country.

Our history

Founded in November 2017 in Tetovo, UNSHM started as a non-profit, independent, nonpartisan student grassroots movement in the Polog region, spreading to the entirety of the country by 2020. The Union unites high-school students of all the ethnicities and faiths of North Macedonia.
